Pac-Man


Pac-Man is a cultural icon whose popularity has crossed the globe for 40 years. This classic game is easy and fun to play!

A 2D platformer game coded in C++ and with Artificial Intelligence

My Role

In this independent in-class project, I focused on coding and troubleshooting. I used A* search algorithm to generate paths for 4 ghost AIs, which has 4 states: Scatter, Frightened, Chase and Dead. These 4 ghost AIs also have their own thoughts when chasing after you!

Features and Demo

The ghosts are cycling between the Scatter, Frighten, and Dead states.
