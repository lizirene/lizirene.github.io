Parkour's Edge


It's a 3D first-person game in which player can run, jump, collect coins and climb the walls along with exicting BGM.
Watch out! Those security cameras are dangerous...don't get caught! Also be careful about those lasers...

A 3D platformer game coded in C++

My Role

In this independent in-class project, I focused on coding and troubleshooting.

Features and Demo

To be player friendly, there is a tutorial to guide you through the tutorial level.
The arrow at the top of the screen points towards the next checkpoint the player needs to reach. The radar at the bottom enables you to know if there are "evil" cameras ahead. The timer and coin collecter also allow you to keep track of your record. You would automatically go to the next level when you finished exploring the current level.
